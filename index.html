Donating to charity is a major mood-booster. The knowledge that youre helping others is hugely empowering and, in turn, can make you feel happier and more fulfilled. Research has identified a link between making a donation to charity and increased activity in the area of the brain that registers pleasure proving that as the old adage goes, it really is far better to give than to receive.
